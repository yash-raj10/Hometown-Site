Attractions in Sitamarhi

Janaki Mandir

Janki Mandir popularly known as A Janaki Sthan (temple with history from 1599) next to Urjiva Kund. The Janaki Mandir is apparently modern and is about 100 years old only. The town however contains no relics of archaeological interest

Sita kund/Urjiva Kund

In course of time, the land lapsed into a jungle until about 500 years ago, when a Hindu ascetic, named Birbal Das came to know the site by divine inspiration where Sita was born. He came down from Ayodhya and cleared the jungle. He found the images set up by Raja Janak, built temple over there and commenced the worship of Janaki or Sita.

Punaura Dham

The Punaura Dham is a Hindu pilgrimage site in Sitamarhi(Bihar), which has an ancient Hindu temple. It is situated 5 km west of Sitamarhi City and a popular visitor's attraction. Punaura Dham Janki Mandir is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Goddess Sita
